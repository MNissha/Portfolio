About Me

I am a UI/UX designer with a focus on creating intuitive and engaging digital experiences. My approach combines user-centered design principles with creative problem-solving to deliver impactful solutions.

I specialize in:

  • E-commerce Design: Crafting visually appealing and conversion-focused online storefronts.
  • Responsive Web Applications: Developing seamless experiences optimized for desktop and mobile platforms.
  • Mobile App Design: Designing user-friendly interfaces tailored for smartphone and tablet users.
  • Game Design: Creating immersive user experiences for games, focusing on accessibility and engagement.

I have a solid foundation in programming, with experience in the following languages and tools:

  • Languages: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, Java, Python, C#, Unity scripting and Kotlin.
  • Tools: Unity (for game development), Android Studio (for mobile applications), Firebase (for database integration), and Figma (for UI/UX design).

With a strong passion for combining creativity and technology, I aim to create products that resonate with users while meeting business objectives.
